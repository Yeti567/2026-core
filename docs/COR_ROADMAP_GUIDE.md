## Recommended Implementation Order

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-2)
**Elements**: 1, 5
- Start with Health & Safety Policy (Element 1) - foundation for everything
- Develop Company Safety Rules (Element 5) in parallel

### Phase 2: Assessment & Planning (Weeks 3-6)
**Elements**: 2, 7, 10, 11
- Conduct comprehensive Hazard Assessment (Element 2)
- Set up Preventative Maintenance program (Element 7)
- Establish Incident Investigation system (Element 10)
- Develop Emergency Preparedness plans (Element 11)

### Phase 3: Procedures & Practices (Weeks 7-14)
**Elements**: 3, 4, 6, 8, 9
- Develop Safe Work Practices (Element 3) - depends on hazard assessment
- Create Safe Job Procedures (Element 4) - depends on SWPs
- Implement PPE program (Element 6) - depends on hazard assessment
- Establish Training & Communication (Element 8)
- Set up Workplace Inspections (Element 9)

### Phase 4: Compliance & Review (Weeks 15-18)
**Elements**: 12, 13, 14
- Implement Statistics & Records (Element 12) - depends on incident system
- Ensure Legislation & Compliance (Element 13)
- Conduct Management Review (Element 14) - final element

## Detailed Element Information

### Element 1: Health & Safety Policy
- **Weight**: 5%
- **Timeline**: 2 weeks
- **Complexity**: Low
- **Dependencies**: None
- **Blocks**: Elements 2, 5, 8, 14

**Required Documentation**:
- Written health and safety policy statement
- Policy signed by CEO/President
- Policy acknowledgment forms from all workers
- Evidence policy is posted and communicated

**Key Activities**:
1. Draft health and safety policy statement
2. Get senior management signature
3. Post policy in all work areas
4. Include in employee orientation
5. Obtain worker acknowledgments

**Success Criteria**:
- ✅ Policy document exists and is signed
- ✅ Policy is posted and accessible
- ✅ All workers have acknowledged policy
- ✅ Policy reviewed annually

- **Weight**: 10%
- **Timeline**: 4 weeks

## Key Success Factors

1. **Start Early**: Begin with foundation elements (1, 5)
2. **Follow Dependencies**: Don't skip dependent elements
3. **Document Everything**: Maintain comprehensive records
4. **Train Continuously**: Ongoing training is critical
5. **Review Regularly**: Management review ensures progress
6. **Stay Compliant**: Monitor regulatory requirements
7. **Involve Workers**: Worker participation is essential
8. **Track Progress**: Use statistics to guide improvements
